My Expertise

App Development

I specialize in mobile application development using Flutter, with a strong focus on creating seamless and responsive cross-platform apps. With over 3 years of experience, I have developed a wide range of applications, from simple utilities to complex systems, ensuring a smooth user experience across both Android and iOS devices. My expertise includes integrating APIs, managing advertisements, implementing payment gateways like Apple Pay, and enabling social authentication such as Apple Sign-In. I am also proficient in deep linking, app analytics, crashlytics, performance optimization, and app security. I am passionate about structuring projects for optimal efficiency and am always exploring the latest advancements in mobile development.

Backend

While my core strength lies in mobile app development, I also have experience in backend development using Node.js and Spring Boot. I've developed and deployed servers for e-commerce platforms, focusing on performance, scalability, and security. Additionally, I've created custom customer support systems to enhance user interaction and experience. My backend work includes designing RESTful APIs, managing databases, and implementing CI/CD pipelines for automated releases. I'm actively continuing to learn and grow in backend technologies, and my projects demonstrate my ability to manage complex server-side requirements.

AI and Chatbot

My journey into AI and chatbot development is fueled by a desire to create intelligent systems that can interact with users in a natural, meaningful way. I am currently exploring open-source AI models like Rasa, where I have developed a custom customer support system tailored to specific client needs. This system leverages Rasa for model training and Node.js as the server, with a Flutter-based frontend. Although I am still expanding my expertise in this domain, I am excited about the possibilities AI offers and am committed to pushing the boundaries of what can be achieved with machine learning and natural language processing. My work in AI is a continuous exploration, and I look forward to contributing more to this rapidly evolving field.
